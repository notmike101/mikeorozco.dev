Description

Unique Fusion of FPS and MOBA (Pre-Alpha)

Join us in the creation of Stories One, where the frenetic action of an FPS meets the strategic depth of a MOBA. In this pre-alpha stage, we invite you to experience and help shape this 5v5 PvP arena. Your aim, your strategy, and your feedback are pivotal to the game's development.

Cooperative Team Play

Teamwork is the essence of Stories One. Assemble your squad, plan your attack on the two lanes leading to the enemy base, and work together to dismantle their defenses while protecting your own.



Arsenal and Special Powers

Choose from five unique weapons and special powers to create the perfect loadout for your playstyle. Coordinate with your team, strategize, and use your arsenal effectively to lay waste to enemy defenses and seize victory.




Strategic Deck Building and Leveling Up

Harness the power of a well-built deck to enhance your character's stats and abilities. Gain levels by destroying enemy turrets, eliminating rival players or their robotic minions, and choose cards from your deck to get the upper hand. The better you perform, the more powerful you become.




Reward System and In-Game Shop

Every victory brings you points to spend in our in-game shop. Purchase new cards, customize your deck (up to 15 cards), and come up with fresh strategies for each game.



Personalized Avatar Customization

Dive deeper into the world of Stories One with extensive avatar customization options. Craft a unique identity for your university student as they navigate the challenges of the battlefield. From clothing and accessories to distinct facial features, make your mark and stand out in every match. Your style, your statement.




Join us on this exciting journey to build a game that transcends traditional genres, blending strategy, team play, and fast-paced shooter action. Stories One is in its early stages and we're looking for players like you to help shape its future. Together, we can craft a truly remarkable gaming experience. Your story starts here.
