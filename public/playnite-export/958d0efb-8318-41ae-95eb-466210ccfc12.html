Description

Bloodhunt is a thrilling free-to-play battle royale game set in Prague consumed by a ruthless war between vampire factions. Use your supernatural powers to hunt and outsmart your rivals and Inquisition soldiers in this third-person action shooter. Gear up, feed on potent blood to grow in power, defy gravity to strike from anywhere, and dominate the night!



SUPERNATURAL WAR
Whether you prefer a sneaky surprise attack or dive straight into battle all guns blazing, choose your clan allegiance to define your playstyle with unique supernatural powers.


RUTHLESS DOMINANCE
Roam from the deadly streets to the rooftops of Prague preying on mortals to grow in power. Finish off or commit diablerie on every enemy that crosses your path to be the last one standing.


UNHOLY ALLIANCES
Hunt as a solitary predator or rally your friends to join you and go into visceral, fast-paced fights where teamplay and strategy are essential.


STYLISH PREDATORS
Create your most unique looks for rivals to remember you by. Customize your appearance with stylish outfits, timeless hairdos, piercings or tattoos, and show off your personality with looks that truly kill.


VAMPIRE: THE MASQUERADE LEGACY
Set in the World of Darkness universe, Bloodhunt draws from this grim version of our modern world where vampires hide in plain sight and fight against hunters, other vampires, and their own worst instincts, all while struggling to maintain The Masquerade.
