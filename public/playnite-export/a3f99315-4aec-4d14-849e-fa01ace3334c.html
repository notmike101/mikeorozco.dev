Description

First Anniversary Since the Launch! Season 7 is Here!

In Season 7, the top-ranked Pro Hero on the Hero Billboard Chart, Hawks, also known as the The Man Who's A Bit Too Fast, makes his debut as a new character! His standout feature is his Quirk Fierce Wings, which allows him to use the feathers for attacks and flight. Experience his incredible flight abilities and speed, which make him effective in both offense and support roles!

Additionally, starting this season, U.A. Island has been partially revamped. While keeping the numerous buildings as they are, we’ve added new features including jump ramps to create a more dynamic battleground.

Moreover, with the launch of Season 7, MY HERO ULTRA RUMBLE celebrates its first anniversary since its release. To commemorate this milestone, special items and decorations have been implemented throughout the stages and various screens.

And that’s not all! This season also brings new costumes and more. Don’t miss out, make sure to get them!


Leap into the fray with your fellow heroes...and villains! Use communication, tactics, and synergize Quirks with your team of 3 to be the last ones standing in this 24-player Battle Royale!





Pick up your favorite characters from a quirky roster divided into 5 archetypes: Strike, Assault, Rapid, Technical, and Support. The strengths of each character are completely different depending on their unique Quirk.





Find items, interact with civilians, and defeat opponents to improve your abilities and get the upper hand over other teams!





Unlock new characters and customization options by gaining experience and completing missions. Participate in seasonal events and try your luck with tickets to glam up with exclusive items!



The game will be updated each season to include new elements, focusing on new characters and costumes!
Jump in and get a hold of the rapidly changing battle conditions!
