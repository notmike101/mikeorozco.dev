WHAT IS SPACEMINERS?


During this Early Access phase, you enrol in the Spaceminers Academy as a Cadet, launching your adventure with Spaceminers: Gold Runner. This initial game mode presents you with a timed racing obstacle course where you compete against yourself and the global leaderboard to get the best time over the finish line.

As autumn approaches, we will introduce Spaceminers: Gold Warrior, an engaging Co-op PVE Arena, and in early 2024, you can eagerly await the release of the ultimate game mode, Spaceminers: Gold Miner, a Gold-Digging training experience.

SPACEMINERS: GOLD RUNNER TOURNAMENT


We will host regular in-game tournaments where you run the course and compete for a place on the global leaderboard to win Real Gold. The winners of our in-game tournaments will get a digital proof of ownership of the gold they have won, which is physically held in a Swiss vault.

SPACEMINERS GAME MECHANIC


Master the art of movement and combat with these core features:

  • Double Jump: Soar to new heights with a trusty double jump.
  • Aerial Maneuvers: Pivot and navigate freely through the air, gaining the upper hand.
  • Stamina System: Manage your stamina to sprint faster, jump farther, and outpace your rivals.
  • The Rockbreaker: Unleash this devastating grenade launcher to clear your path and conquer your foes.
Jump into the action and experience the thrill of Spaceminers!

SPACEMINERS: GOLD RUNNER


The course is divided into eight different zones designed to challenge your skills:

  • Burial Chambers: Master the jump and double jump system
  • Vipers' Pit: Evade deadly fireball obstacles
  • Hades' Lair: Quickly find your way out before the room is filled with acid
  • Boulder Dash: Time your jumps perfectly to avoid being crushed by massive boulders
  • Hoodoo Hop: Eliminate as many enemies as you can without sacrificing speed
  • Laser Chase: Run and avoid the colossal spaceship laser beams
  • Sacred Mountain: Run, climb, and kill enemies to reach the Sacred Temple
  • Sacred Temple: Sprint through the hallowed Temple to collect your Gold Nugget

COMING SOON

The full Spaceminers game is set to launch in 2025. In this final stage, players will employ all the skills they've honed at the Spaceminers Academy to confront the enemy and claim control over the universe's gold.
