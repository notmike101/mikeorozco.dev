Description

Did you know that your pillows engage in ruthless battles when you’re not in your bedroom? It’s true. Also, it’s possible that the pillows you sleep on are already dead. They call themselves Bulanci, and even though they look defenceless at night, experts call them EAP – Extremely Aggressive Pillows.

What does Bulanci offer?

  • A quick, action-packed, funny party shooter
  • Up to 8 players on one device
  • Easy to understand, for both adults and children (7+)
  • The return of a 2001 cult-classic shooter (16 mil. downloads)
  • Possible to play solo/coop in both online and offline multiplayer
  • Customize your character – choose the color, appearance, pattern, and small accessories such as helmet, sunglasses, or headphones with DLC Hero Factory
  • Discover more information about the soft Bulanci nation in the history book
  • Developed for PC, consoles (Nintendo Switch, PlayStation, Xbox), tablets, and mobiles

Game modes

The game can be played in various game modes: Deathmatch, Team Deathmatch, Capture the Flag and Elite Training. The first three will lighten up every party, because you can play them with up to 8 friends on one device. You can also play them online via P2P. Your online score is added up and helps you climb up the leaderboard of the best players.
The Elite Training mode is meant for a single player, allowing you to improve your skills and feats in order to become an experienced Bulanci warrior.

Deathmatch
A battle to the death. It’s not as bad as it sounds, though. Each eliminated player will respawn after a while to keep fighting. The basic weapon has only five rounds and takes a long time to reload. This is why it’s necessary to use tactics and try to get some of the bonus weapons which randomly appear on the battlefield. At the end, the score is calculated and the best player is announced. Also, a matching animation of the Bulanci hero is displayed based on the results.

Team Deathmatch
Purely a team game mode. Teams of players try to eliminate their opponents from the game within a specified time. The winning team is the one that reaches the higher score.

Capture the Flag
Players on both teams try to capture the opponent's flag and bring it back to their base. The first team to succeed wins. Bonus weapons are found in caches on the battlefield. If someone takes them, they secure equipment for their entire team. It’s not always an advantage, though. Imagine trying to annihilate your opponent in a duel, but your weapon is replaced with a mine from a bonus cache…

Elite training
Try to beat your own high score on the training grounds. Your opponents will be 3 tough computer players, but have no fear. There will be several special bonuses to help you get the upper hand. With unlimited ammo, invulnerability shield, bonus time, or invisibility, you will certainly show them what you can do. You can compare your final score with your local leaderboard, but you can also compare your high score from each training map online with other players..
