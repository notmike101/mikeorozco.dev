Description

Join over 80 million players in 1V1.LOL Champions, a Battle Royale sensation where you'll dive into heart-pounding 3rd person hero-shooting action! Engage in lightning-fast battles, celebrate the thrill of a multiplayer game.

NEW: BECOME A CHAMPION!


Discover an array of playable champions in this multiplayer game, including enhanced humans, mythical creatures, and mechanized entities, each wielding unique and powerful abilities! Collect Shards by opening LOL Boxes, upgrade your Champions, and dominate the Battle Royale battlefield.

NEW: BUILD YOUR VICTORY!


Customize your gameplay with your preferred champions, weapons, and armor. Ascend the ranks on the Trophy Road in this gun game, unlock new gear, and gain shards from LOL Boxes to enhance your arsenal with powerful perks and abilities! Utilize your in-game building skills to construct structures, while gaining tactical advantages or surprising opponents in this multiplayer sensation.

DAILY CHANGING CONTENT


Experience daily special events, promotions, and limited-time game modes in this shooting game, including:
• LOL Ball – Unique 4v4 team mode in this multiplayer game, the team that holds the ball the longest wins
• Zero Builds – Test your shooting skills and show off your Champion's abilities without building at all.
• 4V4 Team Dog-tag – collect dog tags from eliminated enemies to score points!
• Warzone Battle Royale – The classic Battle Royale mode, skydive and scavenge for weapons and resources, the perfect competitive mode without any head-start.

1V1 CLASH - UNLEASH YOUR COMPETITIVE SKILLS!


In the exhilarating 1v1 Clash mode, go head-to-head against skilled opponents in pure competitive action, on various maps designed to test your abilities.

PLAY WITH FRIENDS!

Team up with friends in Duos Mode or join a 16-player party for endless gaming excitement! 1v1.LOL Champions offers a range of custom modes for you to enjoy hours of fun with your friends online in this multiplayer Battle Royale game.

COLLECTIBLES AND LOL PASS SEASONS!

Personalize your gear with exclusive cosmetic items, including stunning champion skins, limited-time weapon skins, stylish pickaxes, emotes, and stickers. Season Passes offer limited-time cosmetics, ensuring a constantly evolving experience in this shooting and multiplayer game!

Embark on an adrenaline-fueled journey of competitive gameplay, action-packed battles, and endless fun with 1v1.LOL Champions. Master shooting, building, and outsmarting your foes to claim victory and become the ultimate champion in this unique blend of Battle Royale, shooting, gun, and multiplayer games!
