Description

Scream, gasp, or even whimper into your microphone, and the game restarts. Welcome to DON'T SCREAM! – an Unreal Engine 5 horror experience inspired by '90s camcorder found footage. The challenge is simple: survive a night in the Forest without screaming or dying.

Sounds easy, right? But there's a catch. Time only progresses as you move, and the scares are unpredictable. With dozens of dynamic scares and many locations and relics to discover, each playthrough offers a unique and terrifying experience. Every step you take could be your last... so keep your eyes open and your wits about you. It's the perfect game to scare the pants off your friends and family!

SURVIVE THE NIGHT


Dynamic scares ensure no two playthroughs are ever the same; expect the unexpected.
