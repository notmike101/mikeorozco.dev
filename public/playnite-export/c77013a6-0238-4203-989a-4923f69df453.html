Description


Descend into the treacherous corridors of Clouseau Castle or brave the multi-floored labyrinth of Sinner's End. Prepare a death from above with unique landscapes that express a sense of verticality. Discover hoards of priceless artifacts and conquer the monstrous guardians that stand in your path. Then lead your party to safety, before these walls become your tomb.



Embrace your role as a Fighter, Priest, Rogue, Swordmaster, Pyromancer, Cryomancer, Death Knight, or Druid. With first-person clarity, feel the power of each spell, the weight of your weapons, and the strategic impact of every decision. Charge into battle, unleash devastating skills to disrupt enemy formations, cast life-saving buffs on allies, or use tactical throwables to turn the tide. Dungeonborne seamlessly blends the immediacy of first-person action with iconic fantasy class design for an unparalleled experience.



With over 10 unique weapon pairings, every duel becomes a dance of death that brings a fluid, back-and-forth action combat experience. Parry, block, and riposte in a high-stakes fight where skill determines the victor.



Discover equipment with over 80 item affixes that synergize perfectly with your playstyle, or completely change the way you play. Trade items in a user-friendly auction house, then hunt for dozens of legendary and unique items designed to help you sculpt the ultimate build. In Dungeonborne, the thrill of finding rare loot is accessible to all, not just the most dedicated players.



Step into the competitive Arena mode, where you can challenge fellow adventurers, revel in the thrill of combat, and strategize for victory without risking your hard-earned gear. With Dungeonborne's Seasons system, expect frequent updates that introduce fresh content, ensuring that each chapter of your journey is filled with new challenges and rewards.
