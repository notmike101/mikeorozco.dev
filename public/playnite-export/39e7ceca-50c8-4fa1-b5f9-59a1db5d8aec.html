Description

Time to be a Silly Goose! Immerse yourself in the various environments of the Birdverse of Goose Goose Duck. A game of social deduction where you and your fellow geese must work together to complete your mission. However, keep an eye out for those malicious Mallards and other birds that have infiltrated your team and will do anything to stop you.

Play online with 5-16 players, and work together (or against each other) to complete your objectives - whether that’s living to fly another day, or making sure that you’re the last feathered friend standing!

During meetings, try to trick the gaggle with fluff and deception, or be faithful to your flock with the truth! Complete your tasks throughout the map to fill the task bar, or vote out the disguised birds of prey to complete your mission.

Each Map has a unique set of features and intractability, with Sabotages that can impair visibility, communications, and even perform Environmental Kills. With secret passageways and hiding spots, you can sneak around through Vents, Lockers, Sewers, or Fog Passageways to help you fool the flock.

This Game Includes:

  • Built-in Voice Chat with Proximity Chat option!
  • 50+ character roles including The Pelican, The Canadian Goose, and The Party Duck!
  • 9+ Map locations including S.S. Mothergoose, Mallard Manor, The Basement, Bloodhaven, Jungle Temple and Ancient Sands!
  • 6+ Game Modes including Classic, Draft, Corruption Mode, Trick or Treat, Goosehunt and Tastes Like Chicken!
  • Loads of cosmetics to personalize your goose with Hats, Costumes, Pets, Farts and Player Banners!
  • Private and Public Game Lobbies, as well as a Hanging Out mode held in The Lounge with Claw-Machines to exchange tokens earned in game for additional cosmetics!
  • Seasonal/Holiday Events unlocking Task Race mode to earn special tokens to unlock limited time cosmetics!

Classic Mode/Draft Mode

Complete tasks before being sabotaged, or Kill enough geese to end the mission. If you can figure out the ducks, vote them out of the game!



In Draft Mode, each player is assigned a hidden number. When your number is up, you can choose between one of three roles, or you can choose a random role for the game.



  • Select roles to appear in game that are randomly assigned to players upon Start.
  • Varying Sabotages and Mechanics throughout different Locations.
  • Meetings to discuss and deduce to eliminate threatening avian members.

Hoot & Seek

Hoot & Seek is a Social Deduction Hide and Seek mode. There are three roles: Spotters, Snipers and the Owl. As the hunters, your goal is to locate and take out the Owl to win. As the Owl, your goal is to survive and blend in with the crowd while completing task objectives to win.
  • Requires 2 players minimum, 5 players maximum.
  • Global voice chat, all players can speak to and hear one another.

Tastes Like Chicken

Tastes Like Chicken is a Social Deduction Battle Royale. Protect your team's chicken and kill everyone else. Teammates take turns selecting their roles. Teams consist of one chicken accompanied by ducks.
  • Requires 8 players minimum; 2 teams of 4, 4 teams of 2, or 2 teams of 3 and one team of 2 as viable team options.
  • Mode exclusive role selection.

Corruption Mode

A more challenging and advanced version of the Classic Mode, which introduces the Corruption Mechanic and several Corruption related roles. Corruption tokens can be collected throughout the map to lower sabotage cooldowns, or to power up abilities for the Ducks. For a goose, picking up corruption or becoming corrupted can cause some visibility impairments that can be deadly. Complete your tasks to cure your corruption!
  • Mode exclusive roles and sabotages.
  • Day/Night Cycle

Goosehunt

One Duck is on a Killing Spree, trying to kill everyone before the time runs out. Geese, complete your tasks to reduce the timer, or hide from the Duck. Completing a task will reduce the timer by 4 seconds while alive, or by 1 second when dead.

Dine and Dash

The red team of morphing ducks are trying to kill the blue team before the timer runs out. The blue team is a vulture that reduces the timer by eating corpses and is partnered with a falcon who kills faster than the ducks in games with more than 6 players.

Trick or Treat

Villagers complete tasks to call meetings. Vote out the monster or survive to win. The monster has the ability to turn players into Thralls, turning them to their side. Thralls are powered up by the monster’s abilities for varying boosts in hopes to turn the whole lobby to win.

Cosmetics

Customize your goose with over 1000 cosmetics including Hats, Costumes, Farts, Pets, Death Stingers, Player Cards and Banners! And 20 colors to choose from for your goose base. Express yourself, dress silly, go in character, mix and match!

Achievements

Through gameplay and modes, there are achievements that can unlock additional cosmetics. Earn rewards and reach benchmarks as you play!
