Description

Enjoy the casual and easy TPS game Microvolts instead of hardcore or high stress one!


Due to the third person's wide view, you can quickly grasp and fight on numerous themed battlefields. To win the game, create your own winning strategy by using and switching weapons at the right moment.



Take the high ground with various modes and maps!!


Sometimes, the little things you overlook in your daily life can become crucial keys that lead the battlefield in Microvolts. Take a moment to open your eyes wide and examine places like your desk or under your chair that you've never paid attention to before. In those places, amidst the calm of everyday life, an intense battle like never before is unfolding. Experience and master Microvolts' unique and diverse game modes and maps to lead your team to victory.





Break through the limits of gameplay with the multi-weapon system!!


Melee: Kill enemies surrounding you with your strongest hit. Rifle: the most reliable weapon in your inventory. Shotgun: eliminate irritating enemies who try to get away from your sight. Sniper: an astonishing one-shot kill removes the opponent in the blink of an eye. Gatling: massacre attackers extensively with no mercy. Bazooka: its powerful gunfire aids in getting rid of ruthless specialists. Grenade: use an explosive attack to locate hiding competitors. Master all seven weapons' functions, and you may exceed your limits.
