Description

Galactic Glitch: Prologue is a stand-alone Action-Roguelike game with physics-based combat. It has its own self-contained storyline, and will take about 3-6 hours to complete.

The brainchild of an all-knowing AI, SIREN, you are the ultimate killing machine - forged from the neural DNA of humanity’s best warriors. Will you help her escape, or will you die trying?

What does Galactic Glitch: Prologue include?
  • Experience a stand-alone storyline and meta-progression, that will take between 3-6 hours to complete.
  • Two Fully featured Levels: Fight through unique enemies and environments, while testing your skills against a challenging final boss.
  • Unleash Your Power in Multiple Challenge Modes: Put your abilities to the test in our frenetic challenge modes.
  • Discover NG+ Modes: Want more of a challenge? We've added three game-altering NG+ modes that add new enemies and up the ante.

What is Galactic Glitch: Prologue not?
  • This is not a demo for Infinity's Edge. Although the naming is "Prologue", this is a fully self-contained game with its own storyline and meta-progression, and a prequel to the story Infinity's Edge.
  • None of the content or meta-progression of this game will carry over to Galactic Glitch: Infinity's Edge.
  • This game has different mechanics and gameplay from Galactic Glitch: Infinity's Edge. If you want to experience Galactic Glitch: Infinity's Edge, you can play the demo today!

Head out on a journey that will take you across the universe. Fight in bubbles of glitched spacetime, use the environment to your advantage, leverage physics, unlock new power-ups and tear your enemies to pieces as you chase a single goal: destroying everything in your path!






The world of Galactic Glitch is an intricate net of interconnecting paths. Venture through a constantly evolving web of star systems and explore freely as you chart your unique path through the cosmos.

Explore, loot and fight your way through hostile space. Unlock new paths, upgrade your ship with new weapons and abilities, hone your skills, and destroy anyone that stands in your way. The choice is yours: will you play the prudent explorer or go all out with crazy powers and upgrades?








Galactic Glitch is built on a unique tactical and physical combat system. Wield a powerful gravity gun and use it to manipulate the battlefield. Leverage gravity to get the upper hand and unleash the power of physics against your unsuspecting foes.

Tactics are the only way to survive your journey across the galaxy. Bounce your enemies off of walls, hurl asteroids at them, stop missiles mid-flight and reshape the battlefield. Strategically dismantle foes piece by piece, exploiting their weak spots while avoiding their heavily armored defenses and special attacks.








Galactic Glitch is a game where smarts easily beat firepower. Customize your approach to each fight, uncovering hidden secrets and valuable items across your journeys. Unlock new powers with each run, learn from your mistakes and experiment with creative combinations to become the ultimate destruction machine!








  • 2D Space-Roguelite - Experience exhilarating twin-stick shooter gameplay combined with the endless replayability of a rogue-lite and the heart-pounding challenge of permadeath
  • Physics-based Combat - Wield a powerful gravity gun to manipulate the battlefield, hurl massive asteroids, and turn your enemies' own missiles against them
  • Multi-Part Enemies - Strategically dismantle foes piece by piece, exploiting their weak spots while avoiding their heavily armored defenses
  • Dynamic Exploration - Venture into a constantly evolving, glitched world of star systems, uncovering hidden secrets and valuable items as you chart your unique path through the cosmos
  • Abilities and Items - Customize your approach with diverse upgrade paths in each run. Unlock new Powers and experiment with creative combinations to craft your ultimate build
