Description

Overview

On one snowy night, you awaken with blurry memories in an unfamiliar place. Your hair has mysteriously turned white. Seeking refuge, you make your way to the nearest town, only to be betrayed and cast into peril.

As a forbidden vampire spawn, survival is your utmost priority. Who will aid you in your first days of this dark new existence? Who can you trust? And who will you manipulate to ensure your survival?

Discover the secrets, face the dangers, and navigate the treacherous path ahead in "Snowfall Symphony - A Tale of the Crustallus". Your fate awaits.

Main characters

Meet Mina, our protagonist. One night, she awakens as a vampire with a blurry memory and white hair. Now, she must navigate this unfamiliar world and decide who to trust. Along her journey, she might find a powerful friend, fall in love, or choose to betray those she meets. The path she takes is up to you.

Charlie is a seasoned vampire who isn't afraid to bend the truth or manipulate others if it serves his purposes. While he may appear untrustworthy, his skills and resources could make him a valuable ally for you. Proceed with caution, though, as his loyalty may shift depending on what benefits him most.

Hans, a relatively young vampire, inherited his family's fortune, propelling him into wealth and power. Unlike many of his kind, he chooses to collaborate closely with humans and appears to disdain the brutality of vampire society. However, while he may seem like a potential protector against other vampires, there could be ulterior motives lurking beneath his facade of benevolence. Are his intentions truly noble, or does he harbor a secret agenda?

Meet Reina, an ancient and enigmatic vampire who has weathered numerous hardships throughout her long existence. Possessing formidable power and wisdom, she is a sought-after ally. However, gaining her trust is no easy feat, as her standards are exceedingly high. Can you prove yourself worthy of her allegiance and bring her to your side, or will her elusive nature remain beyond your grasp?

Features

This horror visual novel is primarily aimed at women, though of course all genders are welcome to play!

  • Visual Novel with Multiple Routes: Experience 3 main routes, each leading to one of 19 different endings.

  • Many ways to die: certain decisions can lead to the death of your main character or other main characters – nobody is safe in this game.

  • Choice-Driven Outcomes: Each route offers 4 main endings, influenced by your decisions and your affection levels with certain characters.

  • Romantic Potential: Engage with 4 potential romance partners. Beware, in a vampire world, romance is not paramount and might not lead to a happy ending.

  • Roughly 61.000 words and about 1h reading time per route
