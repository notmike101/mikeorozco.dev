Description

ENTER THE FRAY

Warhaven’s dynamic melee combat rewards new recruits and veteran players alike. Choose from a roster of unique soldiers, each with their own motivations for fighting beneath the banner of Warhaven.

Coordinate with your squad before each battle and outwit your foes with tactical maneuvers. Fight to control footholds and the powerful engines of war they contain.

Anyone who fights can incarnate as an Immortal powerful enough to change the flow of any battle. Seize immortality and swing the pendulum of fate in your favor!

INTUITIVE, TACTICAL COMBAT

Newcomers will adapt to Warhaven’s combat system easily, while masters will refine their skills with every match. Swinging your weapon or raising your guard is as simple as clicking your mouse…but players looking to challenge themselves can uncover the true potential of the game’s fast-paced and nuanced combat.

Develop strategies and customize your playstyle, but above all else—trust your instincts.

SALVATION COMES THROUGH STRUGGLE

Earn glory in bold conflicts across multiple maps. Experience battles on different scales, and jump into massive brawls, tight-knit teamplay, as well as life-or-death duels. Cut down multiple opponents with a single well-placed sword swing or shatter an enemy into an explosion of stone with a devastating blow of your hammer. Keep the pressure on your enemies by taking control of cannons, ballistae, and war machines.


FIND YOUR PATH TO IMMORTALITY

Switch between eight different soldier classes and use their unique weapons and abilities to shape the battlefield in your favor. Every soldier has the potential to incarnate as an Immortal, and each Immortal fights with their own distinct powers. Offer your faith to the Immortal most suited to the obstacles you face—from the devastating swordplay of Martyr to the long-ranged magic of Raven.


CLASSIC GAME MODES WITH A NEW EDGE

Experience Warhaven's strategic gameplay through distinct modes, each designed to test your metal! Fight to conquer and defend footholds in Dominion. Push through enemy lines and hold your ground in Onslaught. Soar through the skies and protect War Machines in Arms Race. Clash to your heart's content in the familiarity of Team Deathmatch, and battle to secure the vestige in Warhaven's unique take on Capture the Flag.
Whichever mode you choose to fight in, always fight for victory!

PLAN, COORDINATE, CONQUER

Teamwork is the key to victory. The more you collaborate with your team, the more you’ll discover Warhaven’s hidden strategic possibilities. Seize Relic Stones while fighting with allies and use them to call upon powerful effects and lead your team to victory.


DISCOVER A NEW WORLD

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of Warhaven, which blends the medieval and modern to create a fantasy world unlike any other. Dive into the rich lore of Herarth and discover its history, which draws from diverse real-world cultures for everything from armor design to mythological creatures.

Assume the mantle of a divine Immortal, powerful deities fueled by the unwavering devotion of their mortal followers. Overwhelm enemy squads single-handedly and take control of the battlefield!
