Description

A very short, 2D side-scroller collect-a-thon game that lets you play as the cute HOLORO mascots!

It's the HOLORO's anniversary day!

It's HOLORO's anniversary day! Ollie, Reine, and Anya want to party, but they don't have any money, so they ask Udin, Mr. Akyat, and Toaster-kun for help.

And so, the mascot trio goes on a short walk~

GAMEPLAY - Play through 3 stages!

  • UDIN stage — Play as UDIN, Kureiji Ollie's mascot. This blue and purple stitched small bear will navigate around the forest with her versatile jumps!

  • MERAKyats stage — MR Akyat is a devoted follower of Pavolia Reine, and this gentle peafowl will follow her wherever the wind sweeps. He can fly, and he will reach the top to help his lady.

  • TOASTER-KUN stage — Toaster-kun is Anya Melfissa's prized toaster machine that somewhat alive. He has a unique way of traversing the stage, powered by electricity and making use of the environment around him.
