Minimalist Box – A multi-level Sokoban challenge

【About this game】

Minimalist boxes is an innovative puzzle game with Sokoban as the core gameplay, redefining the classic mental challenge through four distinct physics rules. Journey through 96 carefully designed levels, experience new puzzle-solving dimensions from traditional logic to unique themes, and feel the intellectual storm brought by each step in the seemingly simple movement of boxes.

▌Four dimensional themes, breaking through the stereotype

• Classic Echo (24 levels)

Relive the sheer fun of the golden age of Sokoban and solve retro-style puzzles with precise step counts using the most basic pushing rules in the grid maze. Each level is the ultimate test of spatial planning skills.

• Double-sided (24 off)

Step into the realm of quantum entanglement! Take control of two different cabinets at the same time and plan the best route in brainstorming! Note: Different chests need to reach different destinations, e.g. red chests cannot reach blue destinations

• Gravitational (24 levels)

When the box has gravity, the place that looks like a dead end may be the secret to clearing the level! Take control of the gravitational boxes that attract each other, make the boxes attract each other, and plan the best route! Note that normal chests cannot be attracted.

• Ice (24 levels, stay tuned)

Predict your predictions on the eternal ice! Once the block is pushed, it will slide to the end of the path, so take advantage of this feature to push the box to the end! Every slip is an irreversible game of time and space.

▌Core features

◆ 96 hand-polished puzzles: 24 levels of progressive difficulty curves for each theme, from beginner guide to brain-burning level

◆ Dynamic physics rule system: The unique interactive logic of each world brings 4 completely different puzzle-solving experiences

◆ Minimalist art style: no bells and whistles, enjoy the most original Sokoban!

◆ Puzzle data analysis: Have a record of the number of steps you have cleared, challenge your limits, and find the best solution in many routes!
